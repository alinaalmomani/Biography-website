A message

Dear Thierry,

You have simply made the Coding Academy a dream come true. And for that, we are ever grateful. It is your vision that shall put us on a continued mission to better the lives of Jordanian Youth. Thanks to you, we shall stand united and determined to empower our youth in every corner of our beloved Jordan. You have firmly rooted our belief in the capabilities of Jordanians and that shall always be the core essence of our dear to heart Coding Academy. Here we are reaping the fruits of our success with 450 beneficiaries enjoying an astounding 80% job market access. And this is only the beginning. Allow us to present this humble tribute to the great achievement that you have coded.

Orange Jordan Coding Academy.
